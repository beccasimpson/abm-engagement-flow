üîó HOW FIT + ENGAGEMENT SCORES WORK TOGETHER

High Fit + High Engagement

  • Fit Score: 100+ pts (ideal account)
  • Engagement: 50+ pts (MQL reached)
  • ‚Üí Immediate RL1 + Priority outreach
  • Fast-track to sales handoff

High Fit + Low Engagement

  • Fit Score: 100+ pts (ideal account)
  • Engagement: <50 pts (not yet MQL)
  • ‚Üí RL2/RL3 aggressive nurture
  • Priority for ad spend, content syndication

Medium Fit + High Engagement

  • Fit Score: 50-99 pts (decent account)
  • Engagement: 50+ pts (MQL reached)
  • ‚Üí MQL but qualification needed
  • Validate fit before AE involvement

Low Fit + Any Engagement

  • Fit Score: <50 pts (poor fit)
  • Engagement: Any
  • ‚Üí Do not pursue
  • Low-touch nurture only, no sales time
MQL + READINESS LEVEL INTEGRATION

MQL as RL2 ‚Üí RL1 Trigger

  • Contact reaches 50+ engagement points
  • Account has sufficient Fit Score (50+)
  • Automatically moves account to RL1
  • Sales SLA clock starts immediately

MQL in RL1 Account

  • Additional contact reaches MQL
  • Sales notified of new champion
  • Multi-threading opportunity flagged
  • Prioritize for outreach

MQL Score Reset

  • Closed-won: Reset after 6 months
  • Closed-lost: Reset after 90 days
  • Decay brings below 25: Move to RL3
  • Unsubscribe: Remove from scoring
