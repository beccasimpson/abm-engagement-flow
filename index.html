üìà SCALE PATH: FROM MANUAL TO AUTOMATED

Phase 1: Now (Manual + AI Assist)

  • Each AE builds individual Claude Cowork skills
  • DJ consolidates into one "super skill"
  • Test on Top 40 accounts per rep
  • Emails drafted by Claude, sent via Gmail
  • Learn what messaging/pain themes convert

Phase 2: Next (Expanded Testing)

  • Apply proven pain themes to ~200 accounts per AE
  • Build standing Unify sequences for top 5 pain theme campaigns
  • Automate RL1 trigger ‚Üí Unify sequence enrollment
  • Multi-thread: Unify targets additional buyers at active accounts

Phase 3: Scale (Full Automation)

  • Apply methodology to 1,000+ accounts
  • Claude enrichment runs on always-on basis
  • Pain theme classification automated
  • Sequences dynamically built from proven templates
  • Rep only steps in when: RL1 + pain confirmed + website visit ‚Üí Slack alert

Key Principle

  • Don't build everything today.
  • Start with small steps that make big improvements.
  • Manual process + AI teaches us what works.
  • Automate only after messaging is proven.
  • Unify is the automation layer for plays developed and tested in Claude.

MQL + READINESS LEVEL INTEGRATION

RL2 ‚Üí RL1 Transition (Behavioral Thresholds)

  • Primary triggers: 1x demo form fill, 1x HVP visit, or 3x non-demo forms
  • MQL score (50+ pts) is a supplementary signal, not the primary RL trigger
  • Behavioral thresholds fire independently of engagement score
  • Sales SLA clock starts immediately on any RL1 trigger

MQL in RL1 Account

  • Additional contact reaches MQL
  • Sales notified of new champion
  • Multi-threading opportunity flagged
  • Prioritize for outreach

MQL Score Reset

  • Closed-won: Reset after 6 months
  • Closed-lost: Reset after 90 days
  • Decay brings below 25: Move to RL3
  • Unsubscribe: Remove from scoring
